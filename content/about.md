Income For Everyone is a daily publication about the collision between artificial intelligence, automation, job security, and income policy.

The site tracks concrete signals: layoffs tied to AI strategy, automation-led restructuring, labor-market data, institutional research, and policy proposals that affect workers' economic security.

The point is not to predict a single dramatic employment collapse. It is to document the uneven pressure already showing up across companies, sectors, and public data, then connect that evidence to the case for stronger income floors, including Universal Basic Income.

Daily posts are short by design. Each one links to source reporting or official data and closes with the policy implication to watch.
